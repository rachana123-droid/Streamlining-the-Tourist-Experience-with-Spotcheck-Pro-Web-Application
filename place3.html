Duration: 07 Days - 06 Nights

Day 01:Arrival at Paris

A journey that provides thrill each day of the trip On arrival, our representative will greet you at the airport. Meet with your English speaking car driver and proceed to the hotel. Our team members will take care of your Check-In procedure. After completing, proceed to the room decorated with dim lights, rose petals and Champagne. You can relax or take a walk in the evening. Visit Galeries Lafayette, champ Elysees and nearby area, also try some local dishes of Paris. Later, return to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 02:Paris City Tour

City tour with a live guide On the 2nd day of the trip, enjoy your morning breakfast and get ready to explore the highlights of the city with a live guide. You will be picked up from the hotel at 1 pm by the guide and head towards the city. You will explore several places like Montmartre and Eiffel tower which has a beautiful view of the city. Every tourist visits Paris to witness the great views seen from the Eiffel tower. Later, you will go on a cruise, where you can enjoy your time exploring the great activities which a cruise offers. Return to the hotel after a fun day and stay overnight.

Day 03:Paris: Day Tour

A day full of entertainment at Paris On the 3rd day of the trip, have your morning breakfast and head out for a day tour. You will be visiting the Montparnasse Tower in the morning which is the tallest building in Paris. The panoramic floor is located on the 56th floor and the view is simply impeccable. The architecture is fabulous and the work on the tower is intricate. This tour provides you with great experience and offers many things to learn. Later, return to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 04:Lucerne: Arrival

A wonderful city which offers many good moments Enjoy your morning meal, check-out from the hotel and get a transfer to the station by a private car. Take a train to Lucerne. After arriving at Lucerne, complete the check-in procedure of the hotel and enjoy your remaining leisure time. You can explore the city on your own. You may visit Mount Rigi which offers amazing views or enjoy a cruise ride on the lake which is included in your Swiss pass. Later, enjoy your night time at the great nightclubs which offers great music to dance on. Later, return to the hotel for overnight stay

Day 05:Luzern: Day Tour to Mount Titlis

A lovely day tour On the 5th day of the trip, have your morning meal of the day and head out for a fun day. Your first stop would be visiting Mount Titlis. Experience an enthralling ride at the revolving Gondola. This gondola ride is one of a kind and as you reach the Mount Titlis, the glacier view will blow your mind. Have a great lunch here and later in the evening, return to Luzern which takes about 2 hrs and return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 06:Zurich: Arrival

A day at Zurich On the 6th day of the tour, enjoy your morning breakfast and Check-out from the hotel and head towards Zurich by a train using your Swiss rail pass. After arriving at Zurich, Check-In in the hotel and enjoy the remaining day on your own. Explore the beautiful scenery or go shopping at the best shopping center in Zurich. You can explore the old town of Zurich which is also known as Altstadt which reflects its pre-medieval history on either side of the Limmat River. Later in the evening, take a free walk by the lake and enjoy the night view. Return to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 07:Zurich: Departure

The final day of the thrilling trip Indulge in a lush breakfast and get ready to pack your bags. Proceed to check-out from the hotel and get a transfer to the airport to board your flight back home.
